=== AFTER DEPLOYING THE FACTORY ===

Call createReactor(coreToken) for each new meme/token:
  - Deploys a new V4BurgersReactor-pattern child wired to the prime + RH V4 infra.
  - Emits ReactorCreated(coreToken, reactor, deployer).
  - Reactor admin starts as the factory (CREATE opcode), factory immediately calls
    transferAdmin(msg.sender) — caller then calls reactor.acceptAdmin() to claim.
  - Registry: factory.reactorOf[coreToken] -> reactor address.
